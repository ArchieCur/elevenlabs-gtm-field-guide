### Three Platforms — One Company

ElevenLabs has expanded from a single voice product
into three distinct platforms. A CSM at Scale/Business tier
will encounter all three in buyer conversations —
even when the sale is focused on one.

---

**ElevenCreative**

What it does: generates and edits speech, music, and audio
across 70+ languages — with the ability to enhance image
and video content from leading models with ElevenLabs'
voice, music, and sound effects.

What the buyer uses it for: voiceovers, narration,
localized content, brand voice creation, creative production.

Who asks about it: Business Owners building content pipelines,
marketing teams, creators, localization leads.

*This guide covers ElevenCreative's TTS and Voice Cloning
capabilities in depth. Music generation and image/video
are out of scope for this guide.*

---

**ElevenAgents**

What it does: enables businesses to build and deploy
voice and chat agents — with the integrations, testing,
monitoring, and reliability infrastructure needed
for production deployment at scale.

What the buyer uses it for: customer service automation,
IVR replacement, contact center transformation,
conversational AI at scale.

Who asks about it: IT Administrators (infrastructure),
Business Leaders (ROI and risk), enterprise architects.

*ElevenAgents is out of scope for this guide.
It is a separate buying conversation with a separate
technical and compliance profile.*

---

**ElevenAPI**

What it does: gives developers direct access to
ElevenLabs' foundational AI audio models.

What the buyer uses it for: building voice capability
into their own products, integrating ElevenLabs voices
into third-party platforms, high-volume programmatic
audio generation.

Who asks about it: technical buyers, developers,
solution architects, IT Administrators.

*The API is the delivery mechanism for TTS and Voice Cloning
at scale. Module 3 covers integration reality for the
most common platforms. Deep API implementation questions
belong with ElevenLabs' technical team.*

### What the Buyer Is Actually Buying

At Scale and Business tier, the buyer is purchasing
access to three core capabilities:

**Text to Speech (TTS)**
Convert written text into spoken audio using
ElevenLabs' voice models. Choose from the Voice Library
or use a cloned voice. Control expressiveness,
pace, and tone. Generate in 70+ languages.

**Voice Cloning**
Create a voice model from audio recordings of a
real person. Instant Cloning from a short sample.
Professional Cloning from studio-quality recordings.
Use the cloned voice anywhere TTS is available.

**Voice Library**
Access thousands of pre-built voices contributed
by voice talent and available for licensed use.
Range of accents, ages, languages, and styles.
*Note: Library voices carry a business continuity risk —
they can be removed by creators without notice and without
a migration path. For the full picture see Module 7
(Limitation 7). For consent and IP questions on
Library voice use in commercial production, see Module 2B.*

### What This Guide Does Not Cover

This guide covers TTS and Voice Cloning for Scale
and Business tier buyers. It intentionally does not cover:

- **ElevenAgents** — separate buying conversation,
  separate technical and compliance profile
- **AI Music generation** — out of scope for this guide
- **Free and Creator tier buyer conversations** —
  different use cases, different objections,
  different pricing dynamics
- **Deep API implementation** — belongs with
  ElevenLabs' technical team
- **Enterprise tier** — pricing and capabilities
  above Business tier require direct ElevenLabs
  enterprise sales involvement

A CSM who knows what's out of scope is protected
from over-promising. When a buyer asks about
something outside this guide's scope, the right
answer is always: *"Let me get the right person
from our team into that conversation."*
